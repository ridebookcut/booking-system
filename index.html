✉️ Email Notification Setup (EmailJS)

Follow these steps once to receive an email every time a booking is placed:

  1. Go to emailjs.com and create a free account
  2. Add an Email Service (connect your Gmail or other email)
  3. Create an Email Template — use these variables:
    {{booking_id}}  {{customer_name}}  {{customer_phone}}
    {{service_request}}  {{booking_date}}  {{booking_time}}
  4. In the HTML file, find the EmailJS Config section and replace:
    YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY → your EmailJS Public Key
    YOUR_SERVICE_ID → your Service ID
    YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID → your Template ID
